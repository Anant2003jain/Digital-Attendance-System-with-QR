Training Information
What is Model Training?

Model training is the process of teaching the facial recognition system to identify registered students. The system uses a deep learning model to learn the unique facial features of each student from their images.

When to Train the Model
  • After registering new students
  • After updating student images
  • If face recognition accuracy decreases
