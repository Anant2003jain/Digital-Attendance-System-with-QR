Testing Information
About Model Testing

Testing the face recognition model allows you to verify its accuracy without marking attendance. This is useful for troubleshooting and ensuring the model is working correctly.

Tips for Better Recognition
  • Ensure good lighting on your face
  • Position your face clearly in the frame
  • Remove glasses or other face coverings
  • Try different angles if recognition fails
  • Keep a neutral expression
Troubleshooting

If the model consistently fails to recognize faces correctly:

  • Ensure you have enough training images
  • Retrain the model with more epochs
  • Add more varied images of each student
  • Check for proper lighting during testing
